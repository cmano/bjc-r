Lab 27 Assignment

In this assignment you'll look at the history and performance capabilities of mobile devices.

Requirements

Mobile devices, as we know them, may feel like a recent development. However, mobile devices (phones) have been around for almost 100 years. Search on the Internet for information about mobile device history. You may only need a few sites to help you complete this assignment, but don't rely on the first ones you find. Many resources are incomplete.

Part 1

  1. Create a timeline, similar to page 1 of the lab, of major items in mobile computing. For each item note, in a brief sentence or two, why it's worthy of being on your timeline. Your timeline can be in list form, it doesn't need to be on a line.
  2. Include devices that introduce new and important capabilities (ex. first phone with a camera)
  3. Include landmark events. (ex. first wireless phone call)
  4. Include important mobile device related items (ex. opening of first mobile app store
  5. Include anything else you think is worthy of the list
  6. Your list should have at least 25 items
  7. Your timeline should begin sometime in the 1920's-1940's range

Part 2

Consider the improvements over time in mobile devices that you learned about in part 1. Add to what you learned about Moore's Law on page 2 of the lab. Write a 1/2 page report about your thoughts on how Moore's Law applies or doesn't apply to mobile devices.
