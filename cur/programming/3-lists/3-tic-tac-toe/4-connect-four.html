Lab 13 Assignment

In this assignment, you will build a classic game, Connect Four. However, it's not a cool Connect 4. It's really just a bigger Tic-Tac-Toe.
I find the little circle confusing amidst all the shapes. Anyone else? --MF, 9/10/18

Requirements

  1. The gameboard will be a 4x4.
  2. Prompt the users for their name, and let each player pick a color or an image for their game piece. You do not have to make a one-player version of the game.
  3. Players take turns placing their pieces. Unlike Connect Four, they do not have to place the pieces in the bottom opening of a column.
  4. Have something indicating which player's turn it is.
  5. Detect wins (four in a row) and a tie. Display an appropriate message in each case. Use the winning player's name in a win message. The messages should be said by another Sprite, not the game board sprite.
