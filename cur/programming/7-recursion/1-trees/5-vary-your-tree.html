Lab 28 Assignment: Vary Your Tree

In this assignment, you will make make changes to the tree procedure to see how you can modify the appearance of the result.

A Bit More Preparation

  1. Experiment! Try varying your tree in the following ways:
    • Change the turn angle.
    • Change the scale factor.
    • Change the number of branches at each level.
    • Change the branching structure.

  2. Change the pen thickness between levels, so that the trunk is really thick and the leaves are thin.
  3. Make a brown trunk and branches and green leaves.
    You do not have to get it exactly like the picture.

    4. You might have found the green leaves harder to debug than the varying thickness. If so, what went wrong with drawing the leaves green? Why didn't the same problem come up for the thickness?

Requirements

You will enhance your tree program by prompting the user for various inputs, then draw a picture based on their input. Prompt for, and use, the following items. Tips on many of them are listed afterwards.

  1. Angles (User enters a number, or selects that random numbers should be used)
  2. Scaling factor (User enters a number, or selects that random numbers should be used)
  3. Number of trees (make a forest)
  4. Gravity strength
  5. Leaf shape (at least 3 options)
  6. Leaf color (at least 3 options, and a random option whee each leaf color is randomly selected)
